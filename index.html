Accident / Injury Forms

Accident/ Injury/ Near Miss Policy & Instructions

Please review these policies and procedures, so you know what to do in case of an accident/injury or near miss.

Incident Initial Report Form

Complete this form FIRST for ANY incidents (e.g. vehicle accidents, property damage, personal accidents, etc.) that happen onsite. This is a quick initial report. More forms should be filled out later.

Accident/Injury/Incident Follow-Up Report Form

Complete this form AFTER the initial report form has been completed for any incident, and once more details and understanding of the event have been clarified.

Near Miss Report

Complete this form ANY time there was a near miss.

Witness Statement

Complete this form if you were a witness to an accident/injury or near miss.

Anonymous Safety Reporting Tipline

If you witness unsafe conditions, misconduct, or have a concern you'd like to report confidentially, you can do so anonymously using the link above. No personal information is required. All reports are confidential.

Safety Investigation Report

For Safety Personnel only - please complete anytime an investigation is conducted.
